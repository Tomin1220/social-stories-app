# Evidence Highlights for Social Story Generation

## 1. Structural Guidelines (Carol Gray / Social Stories™ 10.2)
- **Sentence mix:** Keep a ratio of *2–5 descriptive/perspective/affirmative sentences for every 1 directive/control sentence* to preserve reassurance over commands (Social Stories article, Wikipedia, 2025).
- **Sentence types to weave in:** descriptive (facts), perspective (others' feelings), directive (gentle choices), affirmative (values/rules), cooperative (what supporters do), control (child recall strategies), partial (fill-in-the-blank for engagement).
- **Presentation tips:** One idea per page, literal language, visuals that match text, optional blanks/choice boards for participatory reading.

## 2. Behavioral Targeting (Scoping Review, J Occup Ther Sch Early Interv, 2023)
- Studies (n=56) split into two macro goals:
  1. **Reduce disruptive behaviors** (aggression, protests, dysregulation).
  2. **Increase desired behaviors** (emotion ID, executive function, compliance, social responding).
- Effective stories explicitly state triggers, expected responses, and coping scripts; they often tie to ADLs, social skills, independence, and self-regulation.

## 3. Implementation + Outcome Lessons (ASSSIST-2 RCT Protocol & Summary, 2024)
- **Personalisation is mandatory:** Stories were most acceptable when teachers/parents co-authored with child-specific sensory cues and supports.
- **Dosage/fidelity:** Classroom trial used short sessions (5–10 min) repeated across weeks; sustainability relied on ready-to-print templates and staff checklists.
- **Measurement hooks:** Primary outcome = changes in Social Responsiveness Scale; secondary = reduction in challenging behaviours, improved social-emotional health, parental stress reduction.
- **Low-burden delivery:** Templates + guidance needed so mainstream staff can deliver without specialist support; emphasise cost-effectiveness and consistent formatting.

## 4. Design Implications for Our Generator
- Pre-tag each scenario with its goal category (reduce disruptive vs increase desired) and typical sensory considerations.
- Enforce the Gray ratio by tracking sentence types per slide.
- Surface caregiver/teacher cooperation notes in every slide (cooperative sentences) plus a coping/control reminder (control sentence) to encourage generalisation.
- Cache reusable scenario templates and sentence banks so common requests (dentist, haircuts, classroom transitions) render instantly while retaining personalisation fields for the child description.
